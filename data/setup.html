PoolMaster

First-run setup

1. Connect to WiFi

Pick your home network. Your phone will briefly disconnect from this AP while the device joins.

    2. Set an admin password

    Protects the web UI and OTA. You can skip this — you can set one later.

    3. Home Assistant / MQTT

    Enter your MQTT broker to enable HA auto-discovery. Skip to configure later.

    4. Timezone

    POSIX TZ string. Default is Central European (DST aware).

    All set

    Device will reboot, join your WiFi, and come online at poolmaster.local.

    If it doesn't, your router may not support mDNS — check its IP in the DHCP client list.
